ORALSCREEN-Oral Cancer Detection Using Deep Learning.

Oral cancer is a prevalent global issue, accounting for a significant number of new cases and deaths each year. The challenges of late diagnosis and poor survival rates are prominent due to limited access to specialized diagnosis and lack of awareness.

The application of Artificial Intelligence in oral cancer detection is pivotal. Utilizing Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), a deep learning AI technique tailored for image inputs, has shown promising results.

This approach aims to predict oral cancer presence in patient images, encompassing both oral and histopathological images. The objective is twofold: to enable early detection through a user-friendly web interface and to analyze oral cancer detection using Deep Learning algorithms, enhancing diagnostic accuracy.

The scope of this work encompasses various dimensions. By reducing material costs in low-population areas and decreasing diagnostic reliance on healthcare professionals, the proposed system can reach regions with limited resources. Moreover, its potential for commercialization on a larger scale, coupled with future enhancements, makes it a promising avenue for addressing the challenges of oral cancer detection.

Gas Leak Detection Using LPC1768 MicroController.

Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) is a main source of fuel, especially in urban areas because it is clean compared to firewood and charcoal. Gas leakage is a major problem in the industrial sector, residential premises, etc.

Nowadays, home security has become a major issue because of increasing gas leakage. Gas leakage is a source of great anxiety with ateliers, residential areas, and vehicles like Compressed Natural Gas (CNG), buses, and cars which are run on gas power.

One of the preventive methods to stop accidents associated with the gas leakage is to install a gas leakage detection kit at vulnerable places. This project aims to propose and discuss a design of a gas leakage detection system that can automatically detect, alert, and control gas leakage.

This proposed system also includes an alerting system for the users. The system is based on a sensor that easily detects a gas leakage.
